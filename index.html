How ECFS Mathematical Edge Works

A casino-like statistical advantage applied to disciplined trading—here's how a 0.25R edge per trade compounds into consistent returns.

Success Rate (SR)

≈ 50%

I win 50% of all trades. This means 5 out of 10 trades are profitable.

Risk-Reward (RR)

1.5 : 1

I win 1.5× what I risk. For every $1 risked, I target $1.50 profit on winning trades.

Expected Value Formula

EV = (Win Rate × Average Win) − (Loss Rate × Average Loss)

EV = (50% × 1.5R) − (50% × 1R)

EV = 0.75R − 0.5R

EV = 0.25R per trade

(25% return on risk per trade)

💡 What This Means: For every dollar risked (R), I expect to make $0.25 profit on average. This 0.25R edge per trade (25% return on risk) is comparable to a casino's statistical advantage, but applied to disciplined trading. When applied across multiple trades daily, this translates to a daily edge of approximately 0.25% of account value.

💰 Fees & Risk Controls

ECFS uses a quarterly waterfall fee structure that prioritizes investor returns before any manager compensation. Here's how it works:

The Waterfall (Step-by-Step):

  1. Buffer Gate Check: Is the $1,200 buffer intact? If no, rebuild buffer first—no distributions or fees.
  2. Preferred Distribution (Investor First): You receive 6% of your starting capital (e.g., $1,200 on $20k account) before any manager fees.
  3. High-Water Mark Check: Is the account above its previous high? If no, remaining profits go to you—manager earns nothing.
  4. Catch-Up (If Above HWM): Manager receives catch-up to align with agreed profit split.
  5. Incentive Split: Remaining profits split according to agreement (e.g., 50/50).

Key Benefits:

  • Investor First: You receive your 6% quarterly preferred before any manager compensation
  • Loss Recovery: Manager earns nothing until account exceeds its high-water mark
  • Hard Buffer Gate: No manager fees if buffer is compromised
  • Transparent: All calculations clearly defined in your client agreement

Multi-Layered Risk Management System:

  • Hard Risk Limit: 2.5% daily / 5% weekly risk cap (broker-enforced)
  • Automatic Recovery Protocol: Trading size reduces automatically during drawdowns
  • Buffer Mechanism: $1,200 minimum safety cushion (6% of account)
  • Quarterly Rebalancing: HWM and performance evaluated each quarter
  • Transparent Governance: Third-party custodians, independent auditing, real-time fee calculations

💡 Example: On a $20,000 account with a 10% quarterly return ($2,000 profit), you receive your $1,200 preferred distribution first. Only the remaining $800 is subject to catch-up and incentive fees—and only if the account is above its high-water mark.
