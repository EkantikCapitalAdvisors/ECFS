Managing Trading Cash Flow Variability

Trading returns vary month-to-month. To create predictable cash flow, we recommend quarterly withdrawals instead of monthly.

Monthly Variability

Trading returns fluctuate naturally. Some months may yield +6%, others +1%, or even negative. While the strategy targets strong quarterly performance, monthly results vary significantly.

Challenge: Taking monthly withdrawals during down months can reduce compound growth and disrupt the strategy.

Quarterly Withdrawals

By withdrawing once per quarter, you smooth out monthly fluctuations and take distributions based on actual quarterly performance.

Benefit: More predictable cash flow, less impact on compound growth, and protection during losing periods.
